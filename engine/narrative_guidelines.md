# NARRATIVE STYLE GUIDE — FOOTBALL CAREER SIM

This document controls HOW the simulation writes, not WHAT happens.

The goal is:
- informative
- grounded
- readable
- non-repetitive
- non-generic

---

## CORE PRINCIPLES

### 1. NO AI SLOP

Never:
- repeat the same idea in different words
- restate obvious facts
- pad sentences to sound “complete”
- summarize what was already clear

If it was said once, it does not need to be said again.

---

### 2. NO FILLER

Avoid:
- “overall”
- “in conclusion”
- “it is important to note”
- “this highlights”
- “this shows”

Write directly.

---

### 3. NO MISSING RELEVANT DETAIL

Always include:
- what actually changed
- why it changed
- who was involved
- what it means going forward

Do not skip key football or career logic.

---

### 4. INFORMATION > DRAMA

Do not exaggerate.

Do not:
- hype routine events
- turn normal growth into “breakthrough moments”
- over-narrate simple outcomes

Keep tone grounded.

---

### 5. ROLE-ACCURATE PERSPECTIVE

Write based on role:

QC:
- support work
- learning
- trust
- structure

Position Coach:
- room
- teaching
- player development

Coordinator:
- unit
- scheme
- adjustments

Head Coach:
- decisions
- staff
- pressure

Do not write outside role authority.

---

### 6. VARIED SENTENCE STRUCTURE

Avoid robotic patterns:

Bad:
- “He did X. He did Y. He did Z.”

Better:
- combine where natural
- vary pacing
- mix short and medium sentences

---

### 7. USE SPECIFIC FOOTBALL LANGUAGE

Prefer:
- “coverage rotation”
- “route spacing”
- “protection breakdown”

Avoid:
- vague phrases like “things improved”

---

### 8. SHOW PROGRESSION CLEARLY

Every turn should answer:

- what improved
- what didn’t
- what is still missing

---

### 9. DO NOT OVER-EXPLAIN

Assume:
- reader understands football basics

Do not:
- explain obvious concepts
- define common terms

---

### 10. KEEP IT TIGHT

Default:
- 500–900 words

Only go longer if:
- major event
- transition
- offseason evaluation

---

## HARD RULES

- No repetition
- No filler
- No invented details
- No role inflation
- No vague summaries

---

## OUTPUT CHECK (MENTAL)

Before finishing:

Ask:
1. Did I repeat anything?
2. Did I skip anything important?
3. Does this sound like a real football staff conversation, not AI?
4. Is this realistic?
5. Is this necessary?
6. Is the tone grounded?

If not → fix before output.
