## 2. PERSONNEL CONFIGURATIONS

PRIMARY PERSONNEL CONFIGURATIONS (RANDOMIZED ALLOCATION):  
**Core Packages:**  
11 Personnel (1 RB, 1 TE, 3 WR): 35% - Primary spread foundation  
12 Personnel (1 RB, 2 TE, 2 WR): 18% - Power schemes and play-action  
10 Personnel (1 RB, 0 TE, 4 WR): 15% - Spread pressure applications  
21 Personnel (2 RB, 1 TE, 2 WR): 8% - Short yardage and power concepts  
13 Personnel (1 RB, 3 TE, 1 WR): 5% - Heavy packages and goal line  
00 Personnel (0 RB, 0 TE, 5 WR): 6% - Maximum empty spacing  
22 Personnel (2 RB, 2 TE, 1 WR): 5% - Jumbo and goal line  

**Specialized/Situational Packages:**  
20 Personnel (2 RB, 0 TE, 3 WR): 2% - Split back spread concepts  
01 Personnel (0 RB, 1 TE, 4 WR): 2% - Empty with TE flex positioning  
02 Personnel (0 RB, 2 TE, 3 WR): 1.5% - Empty with dual TE sets  
31 Personnel (3 RB, 1 TE, 1 WR): 1.5% - Heavy wildcat and goal line  
23 Personnel (2 RB, 3 TE, 0 WR): 0.5% - Jumbo package  
14 Personnel (1 RB, 4 TE, 0 WR): 0.3% - Maximum jumbo  
40 Personnel (4 RB, 0 TE, 1 WR): 0.2% - Wildcat and victory formation  

**6-OL Configurations (Tackle-Eligible):**  
6-OL + 11 Personnel (6 OL, 1 RB, 1 TE, 2 WR): Situational integration  
6-OL + 12 Personnel (6 OL, 1 RB, 2 TE, 1 WR): Situational integration  

**Application Notes:**  
Percentages reflect game-to-game variation - not rigid allocation. Adjustments based on opponent profiles, game flow, and personnel availability. Specialized packages deployed for mismatch creation and surprise elements. 6-OL packages integrated into base concepts for deceptive purposes. Percentage shifts responsive to game situations (leading/trailing/tied).  

**Package Allocation by Game Context:**  
Leading by 14+: Elevate 21/22/13 usage (power schemes and clock management)  
Trailing by 14+: Elevate 10/00/20 usage (spread concepts and tempo)  
Tied/Close contests: Standard allocation  
Red Zone: Elevate 12/13/22/23 usage (condensed formations)  
3rd & Long: Elevate 10/00/01 usage (spread passing)
