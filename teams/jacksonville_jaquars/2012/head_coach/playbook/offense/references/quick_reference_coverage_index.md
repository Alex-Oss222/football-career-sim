## Game-Day coverage identification extract

### Coverage Identification & Response

**Pre-Snap Coverage Identification:**

**One High Safety (Cover 1 or Cover 3):**
- **CB Alignment tells difference:**
  - Press alignment (0-1 yards) → likely Cover 1 (man)
  - Off alignment (7-8 yards) → likely Cover 3 (zone)
- **Motion check:** Send WR in motion
  - CB follows → Cover 1 (man)
  - CB stays, defense bumps → Cover 3 (zone)
- **If Cover 1:**
  - Double moves work
  - Option/choice routes work
  - Rub/pick concepts work
  - Attack with best receiver matchup
- **If Cover 3:**
  - Seams are vulnerable
  - Levels concepts work
  - Flood concepts work
  - Attack curl-flat areas

**Two High Safeties (Cover 2, Cover 4, Cover 6):**
- **CB Alignment helps identify:**
  - Press (0-1 yards) + 2 high → likely Cover 2 man
  - Medium (4-5 yards) + 2 high → likely Cover 2 or 4
  - Off (7-8 yards) + 2 high → likely Cover 4
- **Safety depth/alignment:**
  - Both deep and wide → Cover 2
  - Both deep and inside → Cover 4 (quarters)
  - One deep/one rolled → Cover 6
- **If Cover 2:**
  - Verticals stretch them
  - Flood concepts work
  - Attack seams
  - Corners are vulnerable
- **If Cover 4 (Quarters):**
  - Deep posts work
  - Wheel routes work
  - Smash concepts work
  - Attack leverages
- **If Cover 6:**
  - Sail concept works
  - Dagger concept works
  - Attack half-field weaknesses

**Cover 0 (No Deep Safety):**
- **Identification:** All DBs up close, no deep help
- **Usually means:** Blitz coming
- **Response:**
  - Hot routes immediately
  - Slants work (DBs on island)
  - Vertical routes (no over-top help)
  - Get ball out quickly
  - Max protection may be needed

**Post-Snap Coverage Rotation:**
- Many defenses show one coverage pre-snap, rotate post-snap
- **Common patterns:**
  - Show 2-high, rotate to 1-high (Cover 2 shell → Cover 3)
  - Show 1-high, rotate to 2-high (Cover 3 shell → Cover 2)
  - Safeties rotate clockwise or counterclockwise
- **Response:**
  - Trust QB to read post-snap
  - Have answers built into concepts
  - Use motion to force earlier declarations
  - Attack where they're rotating FROM
