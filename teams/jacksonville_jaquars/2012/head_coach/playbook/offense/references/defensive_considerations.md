## Playbook defensive considerations extract

## 6. DEFENSIVE CONSIDERATIONS

Plays designed to attack:  
**Coverage Schemes:**  
Cover 0 (Man Blitz): Quick game, rubs  
Cover 1 (Man Free): Double moves, option routes  
Cover 2 (Two-Deep Zone): Verticals, floods  
Cover 3 (Three-Deep Zone): Seams, levels  
Cover 4 (Quarters): Deep posts, wheels  
Cover 6 (Quarter-Quarter-Half): Sail, dagger  
2-Man (Two-Deep Man): Whips, pivots  
Robber Coverage: Mesh, shallow cross  
Match Coverage: Spacing, china  
Bracket Coverage: Flood, smash  

**Defensive Fronts:**  
4-3 Base: Inside zone, power  
3-4 Base: Outside zone, counter  
Nickel (4-2-5): RPOs, screens  
Dime (4-1-6): Verticals, levels  
Quarter (4-0-7): Run heavy, power  
Bear Front: Trap, wham  
Over Front: Pin pull, duo  
Under Front: GT counter, iso  
Tite Front: Sweep, toss  
Wide Front: Trap, duo

## Game-Day coverage identification extract

### Coverage Identification & Response

**Pre-Snap Coverage Identification:**

**One High Safety (Cover 1 or Cover 3):**
- **CB Alignment tells difference:**
  - Press alignment (0-1 yards) → likely Cover 1 (man)
  - Off alignment (7-8 yards) → likely Cover 3 (zone)
- **Motion check:** Send WR in motion
  - CB follows → Cover 1 (man)
  - CB stays, defense bumps → Cover 3 (zone)
- **If Cover 1:**
  - Double moves work
  - Option/choice routes work
  - Rub/pick concepts work
  - Attack with best receiver matchup
- **If Cover 3:**
  - Seams are vulnerable
  - Levels concepts work
  - Flood concepts work
  - Attack curl-flat areas

**Two High Safeties (Cover 2, Cover 4, Cover 6):**
- **CB Alignment helps identify:**
  - Press (0-1 yards) + 2 high → likely Cover 2 man
  - Medium (4-5 yards) + 2 high → likely Cover 2 or 4
  - Off (7-8 yards) + 2 high → likely Cover 4
- **Safety depth/alignment:**
  - Both deep and wide → Cover 2
  - Both deep and inside → Cover 4 (quarters)
  - One deep/one rolled → Cover 6
- **If Cover 2:**
  - Verticals stretch them
  - Flood concepts work
  - Attack seams
  - Corners are vulnerable
- **If Cover 4 (Quarters):**
  - Deep posts work
  - Wheel routes work
  - Smash concepts work
  - Attack leverages
- **If Cover 6:**
  - Sail concept works
  - Dagger concept works
  - Attack half-field weaknesses

**Cover 0 (No Deep Safety):**
- **Identification:** All DBs up close, no deep help
- **Usually means:** Blitz coming
- **Response:**
  - Hot routes immediately
  - Slants work (DBs on island)
  - Vertical routes (no over-top help)
  - Get ball out quickly
  - Max protection may be needed

**Post-Snap Coverage Rotation:**
- Many defenses show one coverage pre-snap, rotate post-snap
- **Common patterns:**
  - Show 2-high, rotate to 1-high (Cover 2 shell → Cover 3)
  - Show 1-high, rotate to 2-high (Cover 3 shell → Cover 2)
  - Safeties rotate clockwise or counterclockwise
- **Response:**
  - Trust QB to read post-snap
  - Have answers built into concepts
  - Use motion to force earlier declarations
  - Attack where they're rotating FROM

## Game-Day front/coverage response matrix extract

### Defensive Front & Coverage Response Matrix

**vs. 4-3 Base Defense:**
- **Run:** Inside zone to 3-tech, outside zone to edge
- **Pass:** Attack MLB in coverage
- **RPO:** Reads to bubble are good

**vs. 3-4 Base Defense:**
- **Run:** Outside zone, counter schemes
- **Pass:** Attack OLBs in coverage
- **RPO:** Scan box count carefully

**vs. Nickel Defense (4-2-5):**
- **Run:** More difficult, use misdirection
- **Pass:** Attack nickel back
- **RPO:** Great for RPOs, count box

**vs. Dime Defense (4-1-6):**
- **Run:** Attack light box with runs
- **Pass:** All pass game available
- **RPO:** Box should be light

**vs. Bear Front (0-technique):**
- **Run:** Trap, wham schemes
- **Pass:** Harder to protect inside
- **RPO:** Watch for loaded box

**vs. Over Front:**
- **Run:** Pin pull, duo away from 3-tech
- **Pass:** Normal
- **RPO:** Normal

**vs. Under Front:**
- **Run:** GT counter, ISO to weak side
- **Pass:** Normal
- **RPO:** Normal

**vs. Tite Front:**
- **Run:** Sweep, toss to edges
- **Pass:** Protected inside
- **RPO:** Normal

**vs. Wide Front:**
- **Run:** Trap, duo inside
- **Pass:** Normal
- **RPO:** Normal
