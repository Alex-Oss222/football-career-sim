# Program Standards
## The Performance Protocol

---

### Core Philosophy

"Physical conditioning isn't about looking good in shorts. It's about fourth quarter execution when everyone's gassed. These standards ensure you can execute our system at full speed for 60 minutes."

---

## Position-Specific Standards

### QUARTERBACKS

**Body Composition:**
- Body Fat: 12-15% maximum
- Weight Range: Position-flexible, emphasis on mobility

**Monthly Performance Metrics:**
- 40-yard dash: Under 5.0 seconds
- 3-cone drill: Under 7.2 seconds
- Vertical jump: 28+ inches
- Broad jump: 8'6" minimum
- Medicine ball throw (kneeling): 38+ feet
- Flexibility: Full shoulder rotation, hip mobility tests

**Functional Tests:**
- 20-yard shuttle: Under 4.5 seconds
- Pocket movement drill: 10 seconds (custom course)
- Recovery heart rate: Below 100 bpm after 2 minutes

---

### RUNNING BACKS

**Body Composition:**
- Body Fat: 8-11% maximum
- Weight Range:
  - Speed backs: 195-210 lbs
  - Power backs: 215-235 lbs
  - All-purpose: 205-220 lbs

**Monthly Performance Metrics:**
- 40-yard dash: 4.35-4.55 (position-dependent)
- Bench press: 1.3x body weight minimum
- Squat: 2x body weight minimum
- Vertical jump: 35+ inches
- Broad jump: 10' minimum
- 5-10-5 shuttle: Under 4.2 seconds

**Functional Tests:**
- 300-yard shuttle: Under 58 seconds
- Flexibility: Full hip flexor range, hamstring mobility
- Balance board: 60-second single leg (each)

---

### OFFENSIVE LINE

**Body Composition:**
- Body Fat: 18-24% maximum (leaner than traditional standards)
- Weight Range:
  - Centers: 295-310 lbs
  - Guards: 305-325 lbs
  - Tackles: 310-330 lbs

**Monthly Performance Metrics:**
- 10-yard split: Under 1.8 seconds
- Bench press: 425+ lbs (Guards/Centers), 400+ lbs (Tackles)
- Squat: 500+ lbs minimum
- Power clean: 315+ lbs
- Vertical jump: 24+ inches (Centers), 22+ inches (Guards/Tackles)
- Broad jump: 7'6" minimum

**Functional Tests:**
- 5-10-5 shuttle: Under 4.8 seconds
- Hip flexibility assessment (pass/fail)
- Ankle mobility test (degrees of flexion)
- Core stability: 2-minute plank minimum

## Monthly Assessment Protocol

### Testing Schedule (First Monday of Each Month)

**6:00 AM - Body Composition**
- DEXA scan or hydrostatic weighing
- Circumference measurements
- Progress photos (optional/private)

**7:00 AM - Speed/Agility Testing**
- Electronic timing for all sprints
- Video analysis of movement patterns
- Recovery monitoring between tests

**8:30 AM - Strength Testing**
- Core lifts with spotters
- Form verification required
- Joint stability assessments

**10:00 AM - Position-Specific Testing**
- Functional movement screens
- Sport-specific drills
- Flexibility/mobility assessments

## Accountability Measures

### Performance Categories

**Elite Standard (Green):**
- Exceeds all minimums by 10%+
- Eligible for reduced conditioning work
- Recognition in team meetings

**Standard Met (Yellow):**
- Meets all position requirements
- Normal practice participation
- Maintenance program prescribed

**Below Standard (Red):**
- Fails 2+ metrics
- Additional conditioning required
- Mandatory nutrition consultation
- Weekly progress checks

---

## Support Systems

### Resources Provided
- Team nutritionist consultations
- Personalized meal plans
- Recovery protocols (ice baths, massage, stretching)
- Sleep optimization guidance
- Supplementation guidance (legal/tested only)
- Mental performance coaching

### Injury Modifications
- Injured players receive modified standards
- Focus shifts to maintainable metrics
- Return-to-play progressions tracked
- No testing until medical clearance

## Implementation Timeline

**Month 1 (February):** Baseline establishment
- All players tested, no penalties
- Individual improvement plans created

**Month 2 (March):** Adjustment period
- 50% compliance expected
- Focus on education and technique

**Month 3 (April):** Standard enforcement begins
- Full accountability measures active
- Performance bonuses available

**Ongoing:** Monthly testing continues through season
- In-season standards adjusted for game weeks
- Off-season standards maintained year-round

## The Non-Negotiables

1. **Hydration:** Urine specific gravity tested daily
2. **Sleep:** Minimum 7 hours tracked via wearables
3. **Recovery:** Mandatory post-practice protocols
4. **Nutrition:** Team meals attendance required
5. **Testing:** No exceptions without medical documentation

---

## Process Discipline

**Preparation is Non-Negotiable:** Winning happens Monday through Saturday, not just on Sunday. Meticulous film study, focused walkthroughs, high-intensity practice—that's the requirement. If you aren't prepared, you won't play.

**Master the Fundamentals:** We don't skip steps. Technique, footwork, assignments—these are the building blocks. We drill them relentlessly until they are instinctive under pressure. Execution starts with mastering the basics.

**Continuous Improvement ("Good, Better, Best"):** We never arrive. After a win, we analyze what we could have done better. After a loss, we identify the process breakdowns and fix them. The goal isn't perfection; it's constant, measurable improvement, every single day. Complacency is not tolerated.

---

## Competitive Excellence

**Everything is Earned:** Starting spots, playing time, roster spots—nothing is given based on contract, draft status, or reputation. Performance in practice and games dictates who plays. Iron sharpens iron.

**Execute Under Pressure:** We practice in high-stress situations so that game pressure feels normal. Mastering the playbook and technique builds the confidence to execute when it matters most.

---
