## Source extracts

### Opening Script Construction (Plays 1-25)

**Script Segmentation:**
1. Plays 1-5: Establishment package - physical tone, defensive diagnosis
2. Plays 6-10: Dual-back series - personnel mismatches, motion identification
3. Plays 11-15: Constraint plays - countering defensive adjustments
4. Plays 16-20: Shot plays - conditional on field position and score
5. Plays 21-25: Contingency plays - situational responses

**Script Elements Per Play:**
- Primary call
- Built-in audible based on defensive look
- Hot route vs. pressure
- Pre-snap motion indicator to identify coverage

**Scripting Principles:**
- Vary personnel groupings to force defensive substitutions
- Include motion on 85-90% of scripted plays for coverage identification
- Feature both backs early to document defensive personnel responses
- Document every defensive adjustment for later exploitation
- Plays 16-25 are conditional based on field position and score

### Scouting Report Integration

**Defensive Package Identification:**
- Document opponent's base personnel (4-3, 3-4, nickel, dime)
- Identify down-distance tendencies for personnel changes
- Note coverage distribution (Cover 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 0, match principles)
- Track pressure rate and blitz packages by situation
- Map defensive adjustments to motion and formation shifts

**Tendency Breakdown by Situation:**
- 1st & 10: What defense do they run most? Run or pass defense look?
- 2nd & Short (1-3): Do they load box? Bring pressure?
- 2nd & Medium (4-6): Coverage distribution? Pressure rate?
- 2nd & Long (7-9): Do they play soft? Rush four?
- 2nd & 10+: Prevent mode or aggressive?
- 3rd & Short (1-3): Box count? Pressure rate?
- 3rd & Medium (4-6): Man or zone tendency? Pressure packages?
- 3rd & Long (7-10): Coverage distribution? Exotic looks?
- 3rd & Very Long (11+): Rush three? Max coverage?
- Red Zone (Inside 20): Man or zone? Bracket tendencies?
- Goal Line (Inside 5): Gap penetration? Man coverage?

**Motion Response Documentation:**
- Do they adjust front to TE trade/shift?
- Do they follow motion (man) or stay (zone)?
- Do safeties rotate post-snap to disguise coverage?
- How do they handle jet/fly motion?
- Do linebackers communicate motion adjustments poorly?

**Coverage Disguise Patterns:**
- Do they show two-high and rotate to single-high?
- Do they play pattern-match principles in zone?
- How late do they rotate coverages?
- Do they use false pre-snap alignments?
- Can we identify coverage from CB depth/leverage?

**Pressure Package Breakdown:**
- Standard fronts: How many rushers?
- Fire zones: Where do they bring fifth rusher?
- Blitz packages: Where does pressure come from?
- Zero blitz: When do they use it?
- Simulated pressure: Do they show and drop?

**Target Matchups:**
- Which defensive backs can we attack?
- Which linebackers struggle in coverage?
- Can we motion into favorable matchups?
- Which defenders struggle vs. specific route concepts?
- Where are the holes in their zone drops?
