## Playbook.md §1 (exact extract)

## 1. SYSTEM IDENTITY AND PHILOSOPHY

Modern Multiple Spread Attack - A comprehensive offensive system that exploits defensive vulnerabilities through formation diversity, concept versatility, and execution precision. Constructed upon 11 personnel as the primary foundation (55% of overall snaps), with strategic adaptation of personnel packages dictated by down-distance situations, field position, and opponent tendencies. Emphasizes balanced offensive capability within spread principles, incorporating both shotgun and under-center operations.

QUARTERBACK SPECIFICATIONS:  
Semi-mobile, pocket-focused QB emphasized. Protection schemes facilitate step-up pocket escapes and designed bootleg actions. Spread distribution across field depth:  
- Short game (0-10 yards): Chain movement, quick rhythm  
- Medium game (10-20 yards): Chunk yardage, possession control  
- Deep game (20+ yards): Explosive opportunities, shot plays

## Game-Day System §System Overview (exact extract)

## SYSTEM OVERVIEW

**Offensive Identity:** Modern Multiple Spread Attack utilizing dual-back versatility with 11 personnel as primary foundation (42% of snaps). System operates from shotgun (70%), pistol (20%), and under-center (10%) with balanced distribution across field depths: short game (0-10 yards), medium game (10-20 yards), deep game (20+ yards).

**Personnel Distribution:**
- 11 Personnel (1 RB, 1 TE, 3 WR): 42%
- 12 Personnel (1 RB, 2 TE, 2 WR): 18%
- 10 Personnel (1 RB, 0 TE, 4 WR): 14%
- 21 Personnel (2 RB, 1 TE, 2 WR): 8%
- 13 Personnel (1 RB, 3 TE, 1 WR): 4%
- 00 Personnel (0 RB, 0 TE, 5 WR): 6%
- 22 Personnel (2 RB, 2 TE, 1 WR): 3%
- Specialized packages (20, 01, 02, 31, 23, 14, 40): 5%

**Game Context Adjustments:**
- Leading by 14+: Increase 21/22/13 usage for clock management
- Trailing by 14+: Increase 10/00/20 usage for tempo and passing
- Red Zone: Increase 12/13/22/23 usage for condensed space
- 3rd & Long (7+): Increase 10/00/01 usage for spread passing
