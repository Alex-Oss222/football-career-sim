## COACHING PHILOSOPHY

These come from his playing experience. He's seen good coaching and bad coaching from the player side for 12 years. He knows what worked on him and what didn't.

**"People First, Football Second"**
Know your players. If a guy's distracted, find out why before you yell at him about a missed assignment. You can't coach someone you don't understand.

**"Show Me on Tape"**
Stone believes in film. You watch it, you see what went wrong, you fix it on the field. That's coaching. He doesn't overcomplicate it. Watch, correct, rep.

**"Good, Better, Best"**
"Good, better, best. Never let it rest, until your good gets better and your better is best." His operating standard. Good isn't good enough when better is available.

**"Standards Apply to Everyone"**
Including the coach. If you set a rule, you follow it. Players respect consistency.
