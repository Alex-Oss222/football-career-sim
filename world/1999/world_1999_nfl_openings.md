# 1999 NFL COACHING OPENINGS — MASTER REFERENCE
## World State: January 1999
## Scope: NFL only. College openings tracked separately.
## Purpose: Structured retrieval for Interview Module, Coaching Module, and carousel logic.
## Sim Rule: No actual future outcomes. All projections are expectation bands, not history.

---

## HOW TO READ THIS DOCUMENT

Each opening is a standalone block with standardized fields. Fields are consistent across all entries for RAG query reliability.

**Field definitions:**

- **Team:** NFL franchise name
- **Opening:** Role title
- **Level:** 0 = GA/QC, 1 = Position Coach
- **Salary Range:** Estimated 1999 compensation
- **Tier:** Stone-specific accessibility — REALISTIC, REALISTIC-REACH, or REACH
- **Competition:** LOW, LOW-MODERATE, MODERATE, HIGH
- **HC:** Current head coach
- **HC Hired:** Date and origin
- **Staff Build Status:** FROM SCRATCH / PARTIAL OVERHAUL / CONTINUITY WITH CHANGES
- **System:** Offensive scheme being installed or retained
- **Why Open:** How and why this slot exists
- **Stone Fit Score:** 1–10 composite of accessibility + skill match + development value
- **Stone Gap Analysis:** What Stone lacks for this role
- **Minimum Requirements (Realistic):** What the hiring staff actually expects
- **Stone Meets Requirements:** YES / PARTIAL / NO, with explanation
- **Decision Maker:** Who makes the hire for this specific role
- **Reports To:** Stone's direct supervisor in this role
- **Hire Window:** When this role is likely filled
- **Key Personnel:** Relevant players or staff Stone would work with/under
- **Linked Dossier:** Path to the team's full dossier file

---

## TIER DEFINITIONS

**REALISTIC:** Stone's current profile (Level 0, no coaching experience, HOF playing career) meets the minimum bar. Competition is manageable. No extraordinary connection or luck required — just a good interview and willingness to work.

**REALISTIC-REACH:** Stone could get this job, but it requires at least one favorable factor beyond his baseline profile — a thin candidate pool, an HC willing to gamble, or a unique credibility play. Not a long shot, but not a walk-in.

**REACH:** Stone's profile does not meet standard minimum requirements. Landing this role requires the HC to actively break convention — hiring a zero-experience coach into a position-coach role based on playing pedigree alone. Possible but unlikely at most organizations.
